Islam-Expo

Islam-Expo is an event that portrays Islamic culture and Islam. The event, now organized for the third time, has grown into a three-day event and is hoped to attract a bigger audience than the previous ones. The main organizer of the event is the Islamic Council of Finland, the umbrella organization for Finnish Islamic organizations.

The goal of the event is to promote cooperation between Muslims and the Finnish society, to bring Muslim organizations together and to help them develop. In addition, the aim is to foster a stronger foundation for being a Muslim in Finland, demystify Islam, prevent and correct myths and prejudices against Islam and Muslims and promote multiculturalism in Finalnd.

Islam-Expo is targeted for both Muslims and the general public. On Saturday and Sunday, the focus is more on Islam itself and on other themes that are of interest to Finnish Muslims. There is also programme for children so that families can attend the Expo together.
